# Customer Intake Questions

## Basic

1. What problem do you want to solve?
2. What machine, asset, process or operation is involved?
3. What outcome do you want from the first pilot?
4. What would make this pilot valuable for your team?

## Data

5. What data do you currently have?
6. What format is it in? CSV, Excel, JSON, logs, audio, vibration files, maintenance records?
7. Does the data include timestamps?
8. Does it include normal and abnormal examples?
9. Do you have failure/maintenance labels?
10. How much history is available?

## Practical constraints

11. Is the data sensitive or confidential?
12. Can the data be shared for this pilot only?
13. May aggregate profiles be used to improve synthetic benchmarks?
14. Who will review the output internally?
15. What is the deadline or urgency?

## Budget / package fit

16. Is this a discovery check, paid pilot, or real-data evidence pack?
17. Is budget closer to EUR 750, EUR 2,500, EUR 5,500, or custom?
18. Do you need branding, extra data sources, hardware notes, or a founder review call?
