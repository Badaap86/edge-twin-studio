# 15-Minute EdgeTwin Demo Script

## 0–2 min — Problem framing

"Most industrial AI projects fail early because the team does not know whether the available data is good enough, which use-case to start with, what risks exist, and what evidence management needs before approving the next step. EdgeTwin turns that uncertainty into a structured evidence pack."

## 2–5 min — Customer Mode

Show the clean Customer Mode flow:

1. Start
2. Choose / build pack
3. Check data readiness
4. Evidence room
5. Quote / delivery

Say: "The customer sees a simple route; the deeper engineering and approval gates stay behind the scenes."

## 5–8 min — Guided Custom Builder

Show how a customer can choose modules and see price/scope logic.

Say: "Custom does not mean chaos. The customer can configure what they need, while EdgeTwin keeps the scope, risk and claims bounded."

## 8–11 min — Data Quality Gate / Evidence Room

Show data readiness and buyer room.

Say: "The important part is that EdgeTwin can also say no. If the data is not good enough, it blocks stronger claims and recommends what to improve."

## 11–13 min — Quote / Delivery

Show payment/delivery readiness flow.

Say: "This can later connect to Stripe or Paddle. The payment provider handles card data; EdgeTwin only unlocks intake or delivery when the payment status is safe."

## 13–15 min — Offer

Close with:

"The best first step is a Professional Pilot Pack. We check your available data and use-case, then deliver a buyer-ready evidence bundle with risks, assumptions, limitations and next steps."
